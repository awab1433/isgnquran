"And We have certainly made the Quran easy to remember. So, is there anyone who will be mindful?"
(The Holy Quran 54:17)

Islamic society of Greater Nashua is honored to host an annual Quran competition on April 12th from 9AM to 5PM for people ages 6-25 years old. The competition is open to the whole community to participate in. Please join us to celebrate our youth in their journey with the Quran.

Event Schedule

Time Activity
9am - 10am: Registration and Tag Distribution
10am - 11am: Pre-Elimination Rounds + Level 1
11am - 12pm: Level 2 + Level 3 Competition
12pm - 1pm: Levels 1,2,3 Awards
1pm - 2pm: Dhur Prayer & Lunch
2pm - 3pm: Level 4 + Level 5 Competition
3pm - 4pm: Level 6 + Level 7 Competition
4pm - 5pm: Levels 4,5,6,7 Awards

Note: Any level with less than 2 participants will be canceled.

REGISTRATION

  • Paricipant Registation

  • Level 1: Surah Al-Fatiha and surahs from Al-Nas to Al-Nasr (6 or younger)
    Level 2: Surah Al-Kafiroon to Al-Asr (age 8 or younger)
    Level 3: Surah Al-Burouj, Al-Tariq, Al-A'la (12 or younger)
    Level 4: Surah Al-Nab’a, Al-Nazi'at, Abasa (14 or younger)
    Level 5: Surah Al-Mulk, Al-Qalam (16 or younger)
    Level 6: Surah Al-Qamar, Al-Rahman (18 or younger)
    Level 7: Surah Al-Kahf (25 or younger)
